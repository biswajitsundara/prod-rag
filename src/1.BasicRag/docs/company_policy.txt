Travel Reimbursement Policy

Employees are eligible for travel reimbursement when traveling for business purposes.
All travel must be pre-approved by your manager before booking.

Flights: Economy class for domestic travel. Business class allowed for flights over 6 hours.
Hotels: Up to $200/night in Tier-1 cities, $150/night elsewhere.
Meals: $50/day per diem. Receipts required for individual meals above $25.
Ground Transport: Uber/Lyft reimbursed. Personal vehicle at $0.67/mile (IRS rate).

Submission: Claims must be submitted within 30 days of travel via the expense portal.
Receipts are mandatory for any expense above $25.
